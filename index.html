About the Workshop

Craft Something
Truly Yours

Step into our Insadong workshop and learn the traditional art of chopstick-making. Using premium wood and hand tools, you'll sand, shape, and oil a pair of chopsticks from raw material to finished keepsake.

Our instructor guides you through every step — no experience needed. The result is a pair of chopsticks made entirely by your hands, ready to bring to your table at home.

  • All materials and tools provided
  • English-speaking instructor (Korean & Japanese available)
  • Spacious studio for up to 20 people
  • Take home your finished chopsticks

What to Expect

The Experience

01

Choose Your Wood

Select from several types of premium wood, each with its own grain, tone, and character.

02

Shape & Sand

Using hand planes and sandpaper, you'll shape the raw block into a pair of tapered chopsticks.

03

Oil & Finish

Apply natural oil to bring out the grain and seal the wood. Watch your chopsticks come alive.

04

Take Them Home

Wrap your finished chopsticks and carry them home — ready to use at your next meal.
